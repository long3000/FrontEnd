7 methods commonly used:

  • val()
  • text()
  • attr()
  • html()
  • addCLass()
  • removeClass()
  • toggleClass()

1) We can manipulate title using html()

$("h1").text("I hacked your H1 title")

2) We can also manipulate the "li" like such

$("li").text("New Text")

3) We can also manipulate the HTML(similar to innerHTML) like such

$("ul").html("<.li.>New Text<.li>Another text")

4) jQuery click() method is a quick and easy way to add a click listener

$("#submit").click(function())

5) jQuery keypress() method is a quick and easy way to add a key typed listener

$("input").keypress(function(event){ if(event.which === 13){ alert("You hit Enter");} console.log("Other key"); })

5) jQuery on() method is similar to vanilla EventListener

$("h1").on("click",function(){ $(this).css("background","purple"); })
